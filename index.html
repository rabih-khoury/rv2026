The Strategic Bets

Four bets that deepen the moat with every user

Each bet reinforces the others. Together, they create a closed loop from intelligence to conviction to execution — and a network-driven moat that compounds with scale.

01

Verified Performance Reputation

The fundamental problem in social investing is trust. Anyone can claim to be a great trader. We're building the infrastructure that makes it provable — verified track records tied to real performance data, visible to the entire community. When a user sees a trader's reputation on Real Vision, they know it's earned, not curated. This is the foundation everything else is built on: you can't have social investing without first solving the trust problem, and no competitor has.

First verified track records expected to be live and public by Q2 2026.
02

Unified Idea-to-Execution

Today, users consume research on Real Vision and leave to execute on Coinbase or Robinhood. That leakage is a critical inefficiency. We're building a seamless "Trade Now" experience — one-click execution directly from any Trade Idea, powered by brokerage API integrations with partners like Schwab, Fidelity, and crypto exchanges. This transforms the platform from a cost center into a revenue generator while keeping users inside the ecosystem from conviction to action.

Trade execution proof of concept targeted for Q2 2026.
03

Tokenized Portfolio Origination

Once you can verify who's worth following, the next question is obvious: how do I get exposure to their best ideas? Tokenized portfolios bridge that gap. Verified traders publish curated baskets. Users invest directly into those baskets on-chain, with full transparency into performance. It's a new category of financial product — one that only exists because the trust layer underneath it is real. This is where a media platform becomes a financial platform.

Working toward a live proof of concept with real capital by Q3 2026.
04

Extensible AI Persona Platform

Our users don't want to talk to a generic chatbot. They want to interact with voices they already trust — analysts, strategists, and traders whose perspectives they've followed for years. We're building a persona platform that allows multiple distinct AI personalities to draw on the same deep content archive and community intelligence, each with their own voice and point of view. The value isn't any single persona — it's the platform underneath that can scale to many, including partners and third parties.

First persona already in prototype. The platform to scale across many personas and partners is targeted for Q4 2026.

Rewriting the Rules

The infrastructure behind investment products hasn't changed in decades. We think it's next.

ETFs exist because people wanted diversified exposure without picking stocks. Hedge funds exist because people wanted access to sophisticated strategies. Both are built on legacy infrastructure — fund managers, prospectuses, distribution networks, and high fees justified by high friction. But the same forces that disrupted media are now reaching asset management. Community-sourced intelligence can compete with institutional research. Verified track records can replace credentialed gatekeepers. And tokenization can turn a portfolio idea into an investable product without a single intermediary.

The most valuable layer in investing isn't execution — it's the moment before execution, when conviction forms. Every platform below has mastered one piece. Real Vision is the only company that owns the full conviction stack — and it's the layer that makes any of them significantly more valuable.
